Welcome to my new blog space! 

I've transitioned to a pristine, light-themed aesthetic designed for focus and clarity. The *Clear Moon* theme uses subtle glassmorphism and soft transitions to create a premium reading experience.

### Why a light theme?
Sometimes, clarity requires brightness. The crisp contrast of `#2C3E50` on `#FFFFFF` allows ideas to shine.

Here is a snippet of code, looking great even in a light theme:
```javascript
const moon = {
  phase: 'Full',
  brightness: 100,
  glow: true
};

console.log("Hello, Moon!");
```
